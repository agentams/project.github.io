Solan Municipal Council

Solan (M Cl) Municipal Council, with population of about 39 thousand is Solan sub district's only municipal council located in Solan sub district of Solan district in the state Himachal Pradesh in India. Total geographical area of Solan (M Cl) municipal council is 6 km2. Population density of the city is 6352 persons per km2. There are 13 wards in the city, among them Solan Ward No 13 is the most populous ward with population of 5180 and Solan Ward No 05 is the least populous ward with population of 1599. Nearest railway station is Solan which is within the city. Solan is the sub district head quarter of the city. District head quarter of the city is Solan. Shimla is the state head quarter of the city and is 48 km far from here. Yearly average rainfall of the city is 863.3 mm. Maximum temperature here reaches up to 35.4°C and minimum temperature goes down to -1°C

Demographics

The city is home to about 39 thousand people, among them about 21 thousand (54%) are male and about 18 thousand (46%) are female. 82% of the whole population are from general caste, 17% are from schedule caste and 1% are schedule tribes. Child (aged under 6 years) population of Solan (M Cl) municipal council is 9%, among them 53% are boys and 47% are girls. There are 9803 households in the city and an average 4 persons live in every family.
